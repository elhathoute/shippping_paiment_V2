Return policy
If you are not satisfied with your Canada Post purchase, please call 1-877-632-6347 within 10 calendar days of receipt to request a return authorization number. If the item is returnable, unopened, and sent back in its original wrapping along with your receipt, you will be refunded in your original method of payment.You must return unwanted items to the Canada Post warehouse within 30 calendar days of the issuance of your return authorization number. All products must be in their original packaging.
